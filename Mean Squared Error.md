# Mean Squared Error

* `L(y,f(x)) = (1/2n)*∑[y_i - f(x_i)]^2`.
* `∂L/∂f = (1/n)*∑[y_i - f(x_i)]`.
* Properties:
    * Not robust to outliers.
    * Larger errors greater than 1 have more weight than smaller errors less than 1.
    * Easy to calculate derivative.
    * L2; mean.
